## Gamification Mechanics

### The Loop
```
Session Complete
       ‚Üì
Immediate Feedback (pet evolves, island unlocks, badge earned)
       ‚Üì
Motivation Created (achievement, curiosity, connection)
       ‚Üì
Behavior Repeats ("Nour needs me", "Next island awaits", "Family is proud")
```

### Why It Works
- **Pet as Mirror:** Child nurtures pet, pet grows with child
- **Visual Progress:** 36 islands = clear, achievable path
- **Real Emotions:** Family messages are genuine, not fake
- **Agency:** Missions are suggestions, not demands
- **Meaning:** Hero narrative reframes "patient" as "warrior"

## Ethical Framework

### Core Principle
**Vulnerable children deserve technology that respects them, not exploits them.**

### Design Standards
- No exploitation of psychological vulnerabilities
- No FOMO or artificial urgency
- No guilt-based motivation
- No streaks that punish missing one day
- Transparent mechanics (child understands how it works)
- Right to digital rest

### Compliance
- GDPR (parental consent for age < 13)
- HIPAA (medical data encryption)
- Child Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
- Accessibility Standards (WCAG 2.1 AA)
